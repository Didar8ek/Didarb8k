About Grand Lion

Situated between the beautiful Yusupoff and Nikolsky Gardens in Saint Petersburg city center, our 20 rooms offer moments of rest and tranquility. If you are in the city you would probably love to enjoy the ballet at Mariinsky Theatre which is 10 minutes walking away. The hotel provides with free WiFi, free hot tea 24/7, extra bed (on request), pick-up service (on request) and other kinds of service you might need. The restaurant Grand Lion will open its door in May, 2019, so you might enjoy buffet breakfast or a quiet long dinner with champaign and European dishes. Please note that the hotel rooms are in 5-storey building without elevator.
